HOW WE CAME ABOUT

After having dealt with one too many cold, soggy, sometimes acrid & crumpled pies that came in the name of home delivered pizzas, our founder, who nurtures a great love for pizzas and a strong desire for enjoying them in the comfort of his home, found himself questioning and rethinking the entire pizza delivery process.

If we baked our pizzas while delivering, rather than baking and then delivering, would they not taste as good as a pizza straight out of the oven, like in a gourmet dine-in restaurant? And thus the quest began.

ABOUT OVEN TO YOU

O2U, or Oven To You, is a set of processes enabled by a collection of technologies resulting in a platform that allows eateries to deliver a delicious, fresh, hot food experience to their patrons right in the comfort of their homes. With O2U, restaurants and kitchens can cook their food while driving to their customers so that the food is ready just when it reaches the consumer, instead of it being cooked and left to get cold or soggy during the delivery journey.

At MLT, we are passionate about eating pizza the way it’s meant to be eaten: piping hot and fresh out of the oven. Our team of pizza experts, food connoisseurs, consultants, and robotic engineers have worked tirelessly to achieve the impossible and bring the oven to you! Learn more about the O2U process here.

ABOUT OVEN TO YOU

Foodtech Ventures is transforming the ecosystem for food delivery with an aim to bridge the gap between the quality of dine-in and delivery food. Our patent-pending technology makes cooking while delivering feasible and financially rewarding. Our objectives are to enable our food partners to cook the highest quality and freshest possible food on-the-go, while building brand loyalty and value for money on the way. FTV offers several advantages, including operational benefits over the traditional delivery model, a cost-effective system with high returns, and a differentiated edge over competitors. Learn more about how we’re reinventing food delivery at www.foodtechventures.in

GRATITUDE

We are thrilled to bring the MLT experience to you. But doing so meant being blessed with the original inspiration and overcoming many a challenge in its execution and eventual delivery. It is our strong belief that all of these have been made possible only because of divine grace, guidance, and help, every step of the way. This fills our heart with immense gratitude and we feel compelled to express this gratitude through our deeds. While one can spend a lifetime and still not be able to share all the joy that bringing MLT brings us, neither alleviate what we perceive to be all the suffering in this world, we are committed to making a difference, however small, to a cause close to heart.

In this direction, we begin by providing financial assistance to those suffering social and legal injustice, simply because they cannot afford to fight their legal battles because of financial constraints.
